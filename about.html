About Our EFFRS

We provide the only neutral Remedial space for all Debtors to present and discuss their debt-crisis in absolute confidence and where Creditors are able to join hands with the EFFRS Team, to help and fully support them to secure the buy-in from the Debtors.

We are about enabling you to meet your EMI's (Equated Monthly Installments, thus reducing your DBR (Debt Burden Ratio), so you can cope, instead of avoiding your Creditors. Ignoring this call will result in your debt situation spiraling out of control. Many debtors are already at this crossroads with no or little hope of surviving and repaying their debt to their creditors. Remember, this is a commitment you must meet regardless.

Our Vision

We are here to help but we also mentor you to help yourself to enable your full Recovery. This is your life, so you must learn to take control.
