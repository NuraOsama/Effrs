Services for our Bank or Creditor clients are as follows:

1. Our Business Model includes ‘walk-in’ clients facing a debt-crisis, all are welcome.
2. In this instance we would seek your collaboration to review their case and consider a plan for restructure and/or a consolidation, so this would require your co-operation and possible approval in line with your organization’s policies.
3. We would welcome monthly lists of default clients allocated to us, whom we will approach to derive an accommodating agreement to meet with us to discuss their current financial crisis.
4. Our team comprise of fully trained and capable individuals who will meet with clients and execute the procedures to secure new agreements.
5. Our ethic is diplomatic, efficient, and meets debtors with morality, because this model has proven more successful than the general collections policies and procedures.
6. The advantage for you as a bank or creditor is that you recuperate your loan funds without having to submit insurance claims and disburse high collection agency fees.
7. Our Fees are below reasonable in contrast with current collection rates.
8. We are an experienced senior management team, ex senior banker and debt specialist, where our team has been sourced from an external or banking collections background.
9. Our core expertise is in fully supporting SME’s, Company Owners and Corporates with Consolidations, Restructure Rescheduling and Buy-outs where approval is possible, subject to UAE Bank or Lenders Terms & Conditions.
10. We also undertake High Profiled Assignments for huge Corporates or Private Individuals in excess of AED 100,000,000.00 in total exposure.
