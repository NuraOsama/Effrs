The UAE has been overwhelmed with SME misconduct for some time now with the most recent years giving rise to an outbreak in debtors unable to follow through with financial commitments. It has been made very apparent that the only current viable solution is remedial and debt consolidations, as well as restructures which in turn save debtors from negative exposure and empower them with responsibility by creating a better, more reasonable scenario where they are able to meet their financial commitments.

It is futile to direct focus on “why” people are facing a debt-crisis, the benefits of shifting focus to a solution via the involvement of all parties has proven more efficient, this form of action is effective in improving the country’s economy as well as promoting a favourable step towards infrastructural changes within the financial sector.

You become removed, detached from life itself, because of the fear of failure, the fear of facing your problems, thus allowing them to reach uncontrollable proportions.

We are here to assist you in achieving control, manageable outcomes that will allow you to breathe again by lifting a burdensome weight from your shoulders. EFFRS is here to guide you through a step-by-step debt recovery path towards your financial freedom, until you are debt free and that this is our ultimate objective.

What we will need from you is your discipline and unwavering commitment, without exception. As our team is committed to your success, so you must be committed to your recovery, it is with true conviction that I tell you that this journey will be one which is without a doubt life changing for you.
