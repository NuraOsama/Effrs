EFFRS Privacy Policy Statement

This Privacy Policy Statement pertains to our current privacy practices with regard to the information

Acknowledgment of Terms and Conditions

EFFRS LLC Debt Remedial Company is committed to protecting your privacy, By accessing EFFRS LLC Website (www.effrs.com), you acknowledge and fully understand our Debt Remedial Privacy Statement and freely consent to the information.

Participating Merchant Policies

Related Services and Offerings with links from this website, including vendor sites, have their own privacy statements that can be viewed by clicking on the corresponding links within each respective website. Online merchants and others who participate in Collection Agencies services.

How we use information and collect it

EFFRS LLC collects certain information from and about its users in three ways: directly from our Web Server logs, the User, and with Cookies. When you visit our website, we may track information to administer the site and analyze its usage.

Use of Cookies and Web Beacons

We do use cookies to store information, such as your personal preferences when you visit our website. This may include only showing you a popup once in your visit, or the ability to login to some of our features.

Personal Information Users

At times, visitors to our website can register to purchase services. When you register, we will request some personal information such as name, address, email, telephone number or facsimile number, account number and other relevant information.

Use of Web Beacons or GIF files

Our EFFRS web pages may contain electronic images known as Web Beacons – sometimes also called single-pixel gifs – that allow our company to count users who have visited those pages and to deliver co-branded services.EFFRS may include Web beacons in promotional e-mail messages or newsletters

Accessing Web Account Information

We will provide you with the means to ensure that personally identifiable information in your web account file is correct and current. You may review this information by contacting us.

Changes to this Statement

EFFRS has the discretion to occasionally update this Privacy Policy Statement. When we do, we will also revise the “updated” date at the top of this Privacy page. We encourage you to periodically review this privacy statement.

Contacting Us

If you have questions regarding our Privacy Policy Statement, its implementation, failure to adhere to this Privacy Statement and/or our general practices, please feel free to contact us.
