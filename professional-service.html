Professional Service Fee Ethics

  2. Professional Service Fee Ethics

EFFRS LLC, Dubai’s professional Remedial Service Fees are based on a few key factors, which are in turn the outcome of many years of experience within the banking fraternity, collections, auditing, due diligence, fraud specialists and over the past 11 years, authentic remedial services.

Ethics is therefore a key component of our Remedial Service Deliverables, which is never compromised.

The KEY lesson for Debt Recovery, is taking ownership and responsibility, where the entire concept of recovery and money blocks, is based on your openness to releasing money that is due, so money can flow in and out of your life with ease.

Q:How does this pertain to our Service Fees being acceptable to pay by any debtor facing a debt-crisis in the UAE?

A:When you backtrack and refuse to enter into a Remedial Agreement to eliminate your dire debt problems that have saturated you with fear, anxiety, depression, ‘stuckness’ and all the negative emotions and situations you find yourself in, then the outcome is a direct result of what you created for yourself, with respect.

Not approving to pay any Service Fee, means you are not displaying Gratitude, Appreciation and Thanks for the help you direly need, where just there,if you did then you would have opened a free-flowing channel for money to flow into your experience, because this is how it works! When you hold onto a thought of scarcity or reluctance to release money for your own well-being, then you are creating the lack that you continue to experience.

THIS IS THE LAW OF LIFE…

When it pertains to EFFRS LLC levying such Service Fee, then you need to understand that it took us years to acquire this expertise, to build up and establish sound trust with UAE Bank Collections and Remedial staff and management, where you can leverage on this expertise, to resolve your debt problems. You can equate it to you going to a creditor, requesting remedial and it is declined, or you do not secure the projected EMI you requested. EFFRS LLC will review your entire debt exposure and if our internal due diligence is approved, we then present your request with sound justification for the management’s review, taking heed that we do not make demands, because we do not have the authority to do so, but, the entire request is based on our reputation in the UAE, which is almost 20years, not including our Group Chairman, who is a UAE National.

Hence, you do not have the liberty to dump your debt on our desk, walk away, refuse to meet our Service Fee requirements, but expecting miracles, or on your terms, which sometimes sounds like this ‘You first do the job, then I will pay you’, where sometimes we have done the job in the past and once done, the debtor disappears and refuses to pay our Service Fees.

Hard lessons have been learnt but the biggest lesson for debtors is that when you pay for a Professional Service that has saved your life, then you cannot equate a true value to the amount of money you pay for that service. Once you take responsibility for this process, it would have been a lesson that you will not repeat again and for the record, EFFRS LLC does not become ‘another creditor on your head’, we have a concise strategy which eases the burden for you, even when meeting our Service Fee payment and that is what authentic remedial is all about.

EFFRS LLC is known to deliver, but the street is 2-way, which means that your full co-operation is required and finally, you should realize that in life, there are no ‘free lunches’, because you must be responsible and retain your self-respect when expecting someone else to resolve your very dire problems.

We trust that the issue of paying the EFFRS LLC Service Fee is clarified here, and look forward to hearing from you, we assure you of our very best attention and service at all times.
